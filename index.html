Power BI Dashboard for life Expectency Analysis

This project analyzes global life expectancy trends from 1800 to 2020, during which the average lifespan increased from 32 to 73 years. Using data from various countries and regions, it explores key factors influencing these changes, including healthcare advancements, government policies, lifestyle choices (such as smoking and alcohol consumption), income levels, and population density. The study also examines gender-based disparities in life expectancy and identifies actionable steps to further improve global health outcomes, such as strengthening healthcare systems, promoting healthier lifestyles, and addressing income inequality. By uncovering the drivers behind these trends, this project offers valuable insights for policymakers and researchers aiming to enhance longevity worldwide.
